Crime Prediction

In this project, I developed a predictive model using unsupervised machine learning to identify regions with a higher likelihood of criminal activity. I collected and analyzed historical crime data from various areas, applying K-means clustering to partition the data into distinct groups. To refine the model, I incorporated an Expectation-Maximization process, which ultimately achieved an accuracy of 75%. I also utilized Matplotlib for effective data visualization, enhancing the model evaluation process and offering actionable insights for urban planning and law enforcement strategies.
